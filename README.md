# MineTwin

From watts to hash, engineered.

MineTwin explores the real-world engineering constraints of industrial
Bitcoin mining operations — energy, thermals, hardware limits,
and curtailment-aware systems.

## What this is
- Early-stage engineering project
- Focused on modeling & simulation (digital twin mindset)
- Grounded in physical and operational constraints

## What this is not (yet)
- Not a commercial product
- Not open-source (for now)
